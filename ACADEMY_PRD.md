## COPY NOTES

1. **Tone:** Authoritative, direct, engineering-minded. Not "learn at your own pace" fluff. Every line should imply consequence — what you *build*, not just what you *know*.
2. **Avoid:** Words like "easy", "beginner-friendly", "step-by-step tutorials". This is infrastructure language.
3. **Brand voice cross-check:** Copy should feel continuous with the main site ("Manufacture attention", "The machine that runs without you"). Students are building *the same systems* NeuraFlow installs for clients.
4. **Cohort dates and pricing:** All placeholders tagged `[TBD]` — to be confirmed by Zachary before page goes live.
